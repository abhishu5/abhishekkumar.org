Methodology

Evidence first. Findings second.

Every audit follows a strict separation between raw evidence gathering and final conclusions. No assumptions are packaged as findings without supporting diagnostic data.

Core principles

What separates this from generic SEO audits

Principle 01
AI systems average ambiguity
When brand signals are unclear or inconsistent, AI systems default to generic category descriptions. Precision requires eliminating ambiguity, not adding more content.
Principle 02
Extractability over volume
AI answer engines don't reward content length. They reward structural clarity — content that can be extracted and summarised without distortion.
Principle 03
Cross-web consensus is trust
AI systems triangulate brand identity across multiple sources. Inconsistency between your website, LinkedIn, and directories produces hallucinated descriptions.
Principle 04
Schema is reinforcement, not a checkbox
Technically valid schema that contradicts or omits your actual positioning provides no AI visibility benefit — and can actively confuse classification.
Diagnostic process

How evidence is gathered

Phase 1 — Query
Direct AI interrogation
I run structured queries across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude — asking each to describe your brand, categorise your service, compare you to competitors, and recommend you for specific use cases. Raw outputs are recorded verbatim before any analysis begins.
Phase 2 — Crawl
Site structure analysis
I analyse your site's entity signals — homepage definition, about page definition, product page definition — checking for internal consistency and extractability. Each page is assessed independently before cross-page patterns are identified.
Phase 3 — Schema
Structured data review
All structured data is extracted and compared against how AI systems are actually describing your brand. Mismatches between schema intent and AI output are flagged as structural conflicts, not technical errors.
Phase 4 — Cross-web
Off-site signal mapping
LinkedIn, Crunchbase, G2, Capterra, press mentions, and industry directories are reviewed for signal consistency. Each source is checked against the brand's stated positioning and the AI outputs from Phase 1.
Phase 5 — Synthesis
Finding and roadmap construction
Only after all evidence is gathered are findings written. Each finding is tied directly to specific evidence. Each roadmap item is tied directly to a specific finding. No generic recommendations are included.
Evidence types

What constitutes diagnostic evidence

  • LLM outputsVerbatim AI responses to structured brand queries — recorded with model version and date
  • Page textExtracted homepage, about, and product page copy — analysed for entity consistency
  • Schema dataRaw structured data extracted from source — compared against AI interpretation
  • Off-site signalsLinkedIn summary, directory descriptions, press mentions — checked for alignment
  • Competitor dataAI outputs for 3 competitors in your category — used for gap identification (Deep Dive only)
Deliverable

What you receive

A structured PDF report. Formatted for your marketing team or agency to act on directly. No jargon-only findings. Each section includes: the evidence, the finding, the impact, and the specific fix required.
