METHODOLOGY

Evidence first. Interpretation second. Recommendations last.

Every diagnostic follows a strict separation between evidence gathering, retrieval analysis, semantic interpretation, and final conclusions. No recommendation exists without observable structural evidence behind it.

FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLES

AI visibility is increasingly determined by interpretation stability, not ranking position.

Traditional SEO systems primarily evaluated documents. Modern AI systems increasingly evaluate semantic relationships between entities, passages, structured signals, citations, categories, and conversational retrieval layers.

01
AI systems average ambiguity
When websites produce inconsistent entity signals across pages, directories, schema, and external references, AI systems compress those contradictions into broader generic categories.
02
Extractability matters more than volume
Retrieval systems reward content that can be accurately extracted, chunked, summarised, and cited without semantic distortion.
03
Cross-web consistency becomes trust
AI systems increasingly triangulate brand identity across LinkedIn, directories, schema, reviews, press mentions, citations, and semantic associations.
04
Retrieval systems behave differently from ranking systems
AI retrieval environments prioritise semantic relevance, passage clarity, conversational usefulness, and contextual coherence over traditional page-ranking mechanics.
DIAGNOSTIC PROCESS

How retrieval and interpretation analysis is performed

STEP 01

Multi-model interrogation

Structured queries are executed across ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Copilot, and AI Overviews to evaluate how systems classify, summarise, compare, and recommend the company.

STEP 02

Entity stability mapping

Homepage positioning, product positioning, about-page identity, metadata, headings, semantic structures, and schema layers are analysed for consistency and interpretation clarity.

STEP 03

Retrieval structure analysis

Content structures are evaluated for passage-level retrieval behaviour, semantic chunking quality, conversational extraction readiness, and citation suitability.

STEP 04

Cross-web consensus evaluation

External references including LinkedIn, Crunchbase, directories, structured citations, press mentions, and category databases are analysed for semantic reinforcement or contradiction.

STEP 05

Interpretation synthesis

Findings are constructed only after evidence patterns become observable across multiple systems. Recommendations are tied directly to measurable structural failures.

EVIDENCE TYPES

What constitutes valid diagnostic evidence

LLM response analysis

Verbatim AI-generated outputs recorded across multiple systems, prompts, retrieval scenarios, recommendation flows, and comparison environments.

Passage extraction analysis

Evaluation of whether homepage and product content can be accurately summarised by AI systems without hallucination or category drift.

Semantic consistency mapping

Analysis of whether all structural surfaces reinforce the same entity definition, category positioning, and retrieval interpretation.

Structured-data reinforcement

Review of schema alignment against actual semantic positioning and AI interpretation behaviour rather than technical validation alone.

Cross-system divergence

Measurement of how differently AI systems classify and describe the company across retrieval environments and conversational contexts.

Competitive retrieval comparison

Analysis of which competitors appear more consistently in AI-generated recommendations and what structural advantages enable that visibility.

FRAMEWORK POSITIONING

This is not traditional SEO auditing.

Most SEO audits still focus on rankings, keywords, backlinks, crawlability, and publishing activity. Those systems primarily evaluated pages.

Modern AI retrieval systems increasingly evaluate whether information can be interpreted, compressed, retrieved, reinforced, and synthesised consistently across distributed semantic environments.

The methodology exists to diagnose where those interpretation systems break.
