The problem

AI systems don't read websites.
They interpret signals.

Most brands optimised for Google are structurally invisible to AI answer engines. The problem is not content volume — it is entity clarity, structural coherence, and cross-web signal consistency.

  • 01 AI averages ambiguity — unclear entity signals produce generic, inaccurate descriptions of your brand.
  • 02 Answer engines compress information — only extractable, structured content surfaces in AI-generated answers.
  • 03 Cross-web inconsistency causes AI hallucination — conflicting signals across platforms produce fabricated brand descriptions.
  • 04 Schema is structural reinforcement — not a checkbox. Most implementations fail to tell AI what a brand actually does.
