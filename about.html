About

AI Interpretation & Search Visibility Diagnostics Specialist. Independent practitioner focused on how AI systems interpret, classify, and surface brands.

Background

How I got here

I spent years doing SEO at a digital marketing agency. The turning point came when clients started asking why their customer numbers were dropping despite ranking well on Google.

When I investigated, the answer was clear — their competitors were appearing in AI-generated answers on ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. They weren't.

That gap between traditional SEO and AI search visibility is where most brands are losing ground today — and where almost no one is doing serious diagnostic work.

I also noticed that most foreign clients of Indian digital marketing agencies were not getting real value when it came to new-era optimisation — AEO, GEO, and voice search readiness. They were paying for SEO work that ignored how AI systems actually interpret brands.

That is why I built this practice — independent, diagnostic-only, with no execution retainers, no ranking promises, and no generic SEO advice.
