AI Search Visibility Audit

A structured diagnostic report. Not ongoing SEO. Not vague recommendations. A single, clear document showing exactly how AI systems interpret your brand and what to fix.

What the audit covers

10 diagnostic sections

01
Executive Snapshot
A clear summary of how AI systems currently describe your brand — the actual outputs from ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity when queried about you.
02
Entity Clarity Audit
Analysis of how consistently your brand entity is defined across your homepage, about page, and product pages — and whether AI systems converge on an accurate description.
03
Schema Engineering Audit
Review of your structured data implementation — whether it reinforces or contradicts your brand's actual category, audience, and value proposition.
04
Answerability & Featured Snippet Readiness
Assessment of whether your content is structured to surface in AI-generated answers — extractability, response format alignment, and direct answer density.
05
Intent Classification Audit
Analysis of how voice assistants and conversational AI systems classify your brand's intent — whether you surface for the right queries and user needs.
06
Cross-Web Entity Consistency
Review of signal alignment across your website, LinkedIn, industry directories, press mentions, and review platforms — where inconsistencies cause AI hallucination.
07
Content Structure Competency
Analysis of how your content is structured for AI comprehension — heading hierarchy, semantic density, and generative engine optimisation alignment.
08
AI Summarisation Exposure Test
Direct testing of how your brand appears in AI-generated comparisons, category queries, and competitor evaluations across major LLMs.
09
Priority Fix Roadmap
Specific, ranked corrective actions ordered by structural impact. Each fix tied directly to a diagnostic finding — no generic recommendations.
10
Expected Outcomes
Clear articulation of what changes should follow from implementing the roadmap — measurable in AI query outputs, not abstract traffic projections.
